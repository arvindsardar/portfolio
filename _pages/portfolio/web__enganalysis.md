Co-designer & sole developer. The client is an engineering company that needed a modern design, clear UI and on-brand site to present their services.

Built on Wordpress, the theme was created on a bare-bones framework, and utilises CSS-grid, bootstrap and minimal javascript.

The site is fast-loading, semantically built and secure.
