### About Me
I am a creative person. I express my creativity in visual design and art, in communication - crafting messaging with empathy and clarity, and in the languages of computing - html & css, php, liquid, b@se, javascript and others.

Although my career began in graphic design, I've gathered deep experience (20+ years) in UI/UX design, front-end web development and all-round problem-solving, as well as, of course,  traditional graphic design.

I'm a relaxed and friendly guy who designs creative that make sense to developers, writes front-end code that’s faithful to the design and always keeps the user front of mind.

For my creative machinery, we have so many tools to provide pieces of the puzzle. Most modern software follows well-known patterns, so grasping a new tool can be trivial if you can recognise those patterns.

I work with Adobe Indesign, Illustrator, After effects, Photoshop, XD, Figma and others to design identity, branding, motion, marketing collateral, wireframing & prototyping User Interfaces, customer journeys and publishing material.

For web platforms, solutions built on Wordpress, Drupal, BigCommerce, Shopify, Jekyll, Neto, Wix, & Squarespace are some that belong in my portfolio.

I've integrated these with numerous Software-as-a-Service platforms for EDMs, CRMs, analytics, inventory - Google, Mailchimp, HubSpot, Salesforce and others. And of course, underneath all of this is good familiarity with WHM, cPanel, DNS, other hosting platforms and tools like SSH, GIT.

Born in South Africa, I am now an Australian citizen. English is my first language and I'm based in the beautiful Sunshine Coast, Queensland.
